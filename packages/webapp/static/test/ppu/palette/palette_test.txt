This ROM displays the full NTSC NES palette.

The top half of the screen displays the regular palette.

The bottom half of the screen displays the palette with
emphasis or greyscale

The display is in two columns, using the following arrangement:

00, 10, 20, 30    08, 18, 28, 38
01, 11, 21, 31    09, 19, 29, 39
02, 12, 22, 32    0A, 1A, 2A, 3A
03, 13, 23, 33    0B, 1B, 2B, 3B
04, 14, 24, 34    0C, 1C, 2C, 3C
05, 15, 25, 35    0D, 1D, 2D, 3D
06, 16, 26, 36    0E, 1E, 2E, 3E
07, 17, 27, 37    0F, 1F, 2F, 3F


Controls:

Left  - toggle blue emphasis
Up    - toggle green emphasis
Right - toggle red emphasis
Down  - turn off all emphasis
B     - toggle greyscale
A     - toggle forbidden $0D black
